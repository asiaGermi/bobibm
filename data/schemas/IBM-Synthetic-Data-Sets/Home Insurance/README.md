## IBM Synthetic Data Sets for Homeowners Insurance
This Use Case has the following Datasets
1. Insurance Policies: List of policy terms, e.g. covered items, deductibles, etc
2. Insurance Claims: List of homeowner insurance claims -- describing the cause of the damage, the items damaged and their cost, and indicating where fraud exists.
3. Insurance Applicants: List of homeowner insurance applications for policies held by people, along with detailed info on the insured and on the home being insured.
4. Insurance Freetext: List of textual descriptions of damage, special needs, concerns, etc for insur_claims.csv
5. Quakes: List of (simulated) earthquakes (primarily impacting insurance claims)
6. Storms: List of (simulated) tropical storms and hurricanes (primarily impacting insurance claims)
7. Volcanoes: List of (simulated) volcano eruptions (primarily impacting insurance claims). Significant eruptions are so rare that most datasets have none.
